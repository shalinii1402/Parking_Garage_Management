Optimizing Urban Space

ParkSmart is a leading parking management system designed to make city life easier through smart technology and data-driven solutions.

Our Story

Solving the Urban Parking Crisis

Started in 2021, ParkSmart was born out of frustration with urban congestion. We believed real-time data could connect drivers with empty spaces instantly.

Today, we manage over 45 locations and serve thousands of daily commuters, drastically reducing carbon emissions by minimizing cruising time.

The Mission

Efficiency & Transparency

To provide the most efficient, transparent, and technology-driven parking experience for drivers while helping property owners maximize their assets.

The Vision

Seamless Smart Cities

To build a future where smart cities utilize automated mobility solutions to eliminate parking stress and create more livable urban environments.

Smart Parking Technology

We combine IoT hardware with cloud-based software to deliver real-time accuracy.

IoT Sensors

High-precision ultrasonic sensors detect vehicle presence in milliseconds.

Cloud Synching

Data is instantly processed, updating live availability across all platforms.

Digital Pass

Users access data via our mobile apps for a seamless entry/exit experience.
